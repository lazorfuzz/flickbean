Flick Bean Coffee Co.

Started in 2015, Flick Bean was built from the ground up by three inventive young lads. Our coffee is sourced from El Salvador, and roasted in our custom-built roaster.

We customize every roast according to the preferences of our customers. Your coffee won't come off a warehouse shelf. Instead, it is roasted and packaged after your order goes through.

Begin by selecting a coffee bean, your preferred roast, and your quantity. Then choose whether you'd like it grinded, then whether you want it in a paper bag or a mason jar.
